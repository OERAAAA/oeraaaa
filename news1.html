The NIHIL series of albums is intended to explore nihilism through sound design and experimental music. NIHIL Vol.2, the second album in this series, continues the concept and style of the series and is a collaborative creation by artists from China. Each artist interprets the deconstruction of nihilism with their own unique sound. My single, “Derelict Heart,” is also featured on this album.
